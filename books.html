How To Kill Those Who Want You Dead

In the heart of Rajasthan lies the prestigious yet enigmatic Thar College, a place where tradition meets modernity. The serene campus is shattered when a gruesome murder occurs in the girls’ dormitory, sending shockwaves through the student body and faculty. The victim, a popular and ambitious student, leaves behind a trail of secrets and lies that complicate the investigation.

Detective Malhotra is called in to solve the case, with the victim’s roommates, Aisha, Divya, and Shruti, being the first to discover the body. As Malhotra navigates a web of secrets, jealousy, and revenge, the killer roams freely among unsuspecting students. In this gripping crime thriller, trust is scarce, and danger lurks around every corner.
