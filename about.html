About

Sammie is a Nepali-born bibliophile and writer with a deep-seated love for literature that began when she was just eight years old. Her days are often spent nestled in a good book, accompanied by her favorite coffee brew—a combination that fuels both her creativity and her analytical mind.

Alongside her literary pursuits, Sammie is a computer engineering student who channels her technical skills into coding, blending her love for technology with her writing endeavors. Whether she’s developing new software or crafting compelling stories, Sammie’s enthusiasm for both books and coffee is at the heart of everything she does.
